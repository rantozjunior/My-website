For Chiesniey, for Heida, for the other survivors—and for himself—he would not fail. Above them, the rift pulsed. The creatures stirred. The city was broken. But down here, in the darkness, the survivors had a chance. And Beldarmore Valgra was ready to defend that chance with everything he had.
