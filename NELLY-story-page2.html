The sound wasn’t in the air. It wasn’t from anyone around him. It was inside his own head. [Host Identified: Beldarmore Valgra] “What—” he whispered, clutching his temple. Xekara moved ahead of them, silent and deliberate, her eyes scanning the destruction. “Who is she?” Heida gasped. “I don’t know,” Beldarmore admitted, keeping his pace steady. “But if we’re lucky, she’s on our side.” A massive shadow passed overhead. Wings, immense and impossible, blotted out the sun for a moment. Heida screamed. Chiesniey pulled them both down, pressing them flat against the asphalt. Beldarmore’s mind raced. This is insane. I’m going to die. I’m going to—wait… no, I survive. [First Quest: Survive] .

Bigger than anything they had seen, wings stretching wider than the street itself, claws tearing at buildings. The smell of ozone and burning concrete filled the air. Xekara’s violet eyes met his for a fleeting moment. Something unspoken passed between them: warning, promise, challenge. Beldarmore felt an odd thrill beneath the terror..
