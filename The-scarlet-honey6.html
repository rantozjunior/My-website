Chapter 2: The Skeem Encounter
(Continued)

Ida smiled politely and entered the elevator. Her heart pounded as the numbers climbed — 1... 2... 3... The doors opened to the same floor she’d seen before — elegant, quiet, powerful. She was shown to her desk — small but clean, tucked near the window where she could see the entire city stretching out like a promise. She sat down, taking it all in. A shadow fell across her desk — she looked up and saw Raim Skeem standing there, holding a cup of coffee. “Welcome to the team, Miss Mbona,” he said, his voice calm but kind. “Thank you, sir,” she replied softly. He smiled faintly and turned away — just as Damon Skeem appeared behind him, grinning. “Well, well. So they actually hired you.” “Yes, sir,” Ida said cautiously..
.
.
“Don’t call me sir. Makes me sound old,” Damon said with a smirk. “You’ll get used to this place. Just… watch your back. Not everyone smiles for the right reasons.” Raim gave him a look, but Damon just winked and walked away. Ida watched them both — two brothers, the same blood, yet completely different. Angled her right into their world. As she turned back to her desk, the faintest smile crossed her lips.
